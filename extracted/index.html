1. Executive Summary

1.1 Project Overview

This Business Requirements Document (BRD) outlines the requirements for a note-taking application that integrates with SAP's CRM system. The application will enable sales representatives, business development representatives (BDRs), and support personnel to efficiently capture, organize, and leverage meeting notes and customer interactions. The application features AI-powered capabilities to enhance productivity and streamline follow-up actions.

1.2 Business Objectives

The primary business objectives of this application are to:

  • Improve the quality and consistency of account notes across the organization
  • Reduce the time spent on administrative tasks related to note-taking and follow-up actions
  • Enhance customer engagement through more effective follow-up and action item management
  • Provide better visibility into customer interactions and prospect activities
  • Leverage AI technology to automate routine tasks and extract valuable insights from notes

1.3 Expected Benefits

The implementation of this application is expected to deliver the following benefits:

  • 30% reduction in time spent on administrative tasks related to note-taking
  • Improved follow-up on action items, leading to a 15% increase in sales conversion rates
  • Enhanced customer satisfaction through more consistent and timely follow-up
  • Better knowledge sharing across teams working with the same accounts
  • Data-driven insights from note analysis to inform sales and support strategies
  • Streamlined workflow between note-taking and action execution

1.4 Key Stakeholders

  • Sales Department
  • Business Development Representatives (BDRs)
  • Customer Support Team
  • Account Management Team
  • IT Department
  • Data Security and Compliance Team
